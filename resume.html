About

Ali Farnoud is a senior Data Scientist at Boehringer Ingelheim. He received his PhD from Heidelberg University. Afterwards, he was a postdoctoral researcher in Helmholtz Munich in the department of Computational Biology and the German Lung Research Center. Ali is currently working as a senior clinical data scientist at Boehringer Ingelheim and is a co-founder and CTO at PolarisAI Fintech company.

Summary of Qualifications

  • Experienced in clinical data sciences, biostatistics, machine learning (ML), and PKPD modeling and simulations.
  • Managed cross-functional projects, driving innovation in quantitative clinical pharmacology.
  • Passionate about leveraging scientific methodologies and advanced technologies to accelerate clinical drug development.
  • Software development lead, cloud computing, GxP computer systems validation, business system leadership.
