Ali Farnoud

About

Ali Farnoud is a senior Data Scientist/Engineer in a pharmaceutical company. He received his PhD from Heidelberg University. Afterwards, he was a postdoctoral researcher in Helmholtz Munich in the department of Computational Biology and the German Lung Research Center. Ali is currently working as a senior clinical data scientist at Boehringer Ingelheim and is a co-founder and CTO at PolarisAI Fintech company.
